What is aypage?

  • aypage is an internet related service, available in Web, Desktop App, and Mobile App allowing you to create, find, share and follow links based on your interests

What is a link ?

  • a link is a combination of related URLs and internet services ( icluding Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus, LinkedIN...) based on certain content or person
  • these links can be categorised as user, company and general links

why use aypage?

  • aypage brings to you the power to to manage your key internet activities with your services (including email, social services, cloud storage service) much more easily
  • aypage makes it easier for you to navigate between those services and it keeps notifying you about them without actualy having to open the service first
  • more intresting is that aypage can let you follow your friends, and able to get updated what they upto on facebook, twitter, google +... on your aypage timeline

how does this 'link', 'following' theory works on aypage ?

As of basic aypage functionality, a user or enterprise creates a link in which other users will link it to follow it, meaning by pressing the button in this way a user adds the link on his/her aypage. This link can be listed on specific chosen section i.e more links, user links, groups etc

Once the link is added, now you are following the link, meaning you will get facebook posts, tweets i.e (depends on the type of the link) on your aypage timeline

Note some of the links might not have social networks services embedded to them in this way they will not pass updates to your aypage timeline. They will only be listed
